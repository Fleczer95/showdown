ShowDown brings the thrill of primetime TV game shows to your pocket — three complete quiz formats you can play solo, anytime, on a single phone. No controllers. No Wi-Fi to play. No setup. Just tap and play.

THREE GAME SHOWS IN ONE APP

The Ladder
Climb a 15-question trivia ladder where every rung gets harder. One wrong answer ends your run — unless you spend a lifeline. Use 50:50, Ask the Audience, or Skip to reach the very top.

The Drop
You start with a million in cash. Each round, spread your bank across the answers you trust — wrong answers drop away, right ones survive. Protect your fortune through 9 nerve-shredding rounds.

The Wheel
Spin for a multiplier, guess letters, and solve the hidden word puzzle. Buy a vowel, dodge Bankrupt, and crack the phrase before your luck runs out.

CHALLENGE YOUR FRIENDS
Set a score you think nobody can beat? Send a challenge link and dare a friend to top your run on the exact same questions. Asynchronous, head-to-head bragging rights — no accounts, no lobbies, no waiting around.

CLIMB THE RANKINGS
Post your best runs to the leaderboards and see how you stack up against players everywhere.
