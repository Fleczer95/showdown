# Research Findings

## App Identity
- **Name:** ShowDown: TV Quiz Party Games (ASO optimized for 30-char limit)
- **Visual Style:** 2.5D Isometric "Studio Interface" focus.

## Technical Stack
- **Framework:** React Native.
- **State Management:** XState (State Machines) for robust game flow control.
- **Graphics/Animation:** react-native-skia (lighting/VFX) and react-native-reanimated (smooth transitions).
- **Social:** Local Pass-and-Play (Phase 3) and Deep Linking for async challenges.
- **AI-Generated Content Pipeline:** Using LLMs to generate curated, game-specific question packs and simulated "100 people surveyed" poll data.
- **Distribution Model:** Monolithic bundle for MVP (initial packs included) with IAP for additional "Extra Packs."
- **Progression Loop:** XP system and a 2.5D "Trophy Room" for milestone achievements.
- **Monetization:** Premium IAP Only (No Ads). Buy "Extra Packs" and "Cosmetic Themes".
- **Localization (i18n):** Full support for English (EN) and Polish (PL) languages. AI Pipeline must support generating content in both languages.
