# Future Enhancements

These features are considered out of scope for the MVP but are planned for future releases.

## Content & Engagement
- **The "Daily Show" Model:** One free "Daily Pack" fetched every 24 hours to drive daily active users (DAU).
- **Global Leaderboards:** Compete with players worldwide.
- **AI-Voice Hosting:** Real-time commentary using text-to-speech.

## Technical
- **Synchronous Online Multiplayer:** Real-time competitive rounds via WebSockets.
- **User-Generated Content:** Allow users to create and share their own packs.
